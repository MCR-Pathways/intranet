Recommendations

Priority 1: Pink discrepancy (the only one that matters right now)

  • Codebase: #FF82B2 — a light, bubblegum pink
  • Guidelines: #DA417C — a deep, rich magenta-pink
  • This is the only discrepancy that affects live UI — it powers the --action token (sign-out button, special CTAs)
  • The guidelines version (#DA417C) has better contrast ratios against white text (4.04:1 vs 2.38:1 for #FF82B2)
  • Decision needed: Adopt guidelines pink or keep current?
