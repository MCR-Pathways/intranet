About MCR Pathways

Organisation · Updated 10 Mar 2026

Our Mission

MCR Pathways exists to ensure that care-experienced and disadvantaged young people have the same educational outcomes, career opportunities, and life chances as their peers. We do this through our unique, evidence-based mentoring programme, which operates within schools across Scotland.

How We Work

Our approach centres on one-to-one, school-based mentoring. Each young person is matched with a trained volunteer mentor who meets them weekly during the school day. This consistent, reliable relationship provides the stability and support that many care-experienced young people need.

Programme Delivery

We work directly with local authorities and schools to embed our programme into the school timetable. Pathways Coordinators — often employed by the school but trained by MCR — manage the day-to-day delivery, including:

  • Identifying and referring young people who would benefit from mentoring
  • Matching young people with appropriate mentors based on interests and needs
  • Providing ongoing support to both mentors and mentees
  • Tracking progress and reporting on outcomes

Our Impact

Since our founding, we have supported thousands of young people across Scotland. Our evidence base demonstrates significant improvements in attendance, attainment, confidence, and post-school destinations for young people in our programme.

Metric Before MCR After MCR Change
School attendance 78% 91% +13pp
Positive destinations 65% 85% +20pp
Confidence rating 3.2/5 4.1/5 +0.9

Our Values

Everything we do is guided by five core values that inform how we work with young people, schools, and each other:

  • Relationships first — We believe in the transformative power of genuine human connection
  • Equity and inclusion — Every young person deserves the same opportunities regardless of their background
  • Evidence-based practice — We measure our impact and continuously improve our approach
  • Collaboration — We work alongside schools, local authorities, and communities
  • Ambition — We believe in the potential of every young person we support

Key Contacts

For questions about MCR Pathways, reach out to your line manager or contact the operations team directly.
